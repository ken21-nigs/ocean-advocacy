About the Advocacy

The "Keep the Sea Fresh & Free" advocacy aims to raise awareness about the importance of protecting our oceans and marine life. Oceans cover 70% of the Earth's surface, produce 50% of our oxygen, and play a crucial role in regulating climate. However, they face severe threats from pollution, especially plastic waste, with around 8 million tons entering the oceans annually.
